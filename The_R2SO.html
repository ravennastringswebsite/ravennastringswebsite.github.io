The Ravenna Second Strings

   
The Ravenna Second Strings is conducted by Nathan Harrison . It is on an intermediate level and is usually a little easier than the Ravenna Strings. It provides a rewarding way to improve your technique and ensemble playing. There are no auditions and all ages (over 13) are invited to participate. Repertoire is chosen from arrangements of medium difficulty or compositions written for intermediate players. We have fun while preparing for the short concerts at the end of each quarter.

Rehearsals are on Monday evenings, 7:00 - 8:15 p.m., at the Phinney Center 6532 Phinney Ave. N, Seattle.

There is a fee of $150/term regular, $90/term reduced (student, senior, etc.) There is also a 'Pay what you wish' option. No one will ever not be allowed to participate due to finances. REGISTER
