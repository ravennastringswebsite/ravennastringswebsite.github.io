Concerts

   
The concerts are free and feature both orchestras.  The public is invited to attend these concerts and to enjoy the refreshments afterwards. The location is Eckstein Middle School, 3003 NE 75th St. If you enter through the main door you will easily find the auditorium. Concerts start at 8 p.m. Note that this is a different place from the rehearsal location. For the date of the next concert go to Updates.
