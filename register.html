Welcome to Ravenna Strings!

To register, click on the 'Pay Now' button below. You will be taken to Stripe for payment.

The Regular fee for the term is $150.00 (this is the default amount).

The suggested Reduced (senior, student, etc.) fee for the term is $90.00. To pay this amount, click the 'Change amount' link and enter an amount.

Similarly, to pay a different amount- more or less, for whatever reason (e.g. to pay for fewer sessions or if you would like to help sponsor a player with an additional $ amount) click the 'Change amount' link and enter an amount.

Please be sure to include your NAME, INSTRUMENT and ORCHESTRA (Monday or Thursday).
