String-along at Folklife, 2012
   
Plan on joining us!

The Ravenna String Orchestras and Friends will have a sight-reading session of string orchestra music based on folk tunes.

This workshop will take place on Monday, May 28 (Memorial Day), at Northwest Folklife. We will set up promptly at 10:30 in the morning, and play from 10:45 to 11: 45 am. Find us at the Seattle Center Intiman Courtyard which is the entrance to the Intiman Theatre, 20l Mercer St. Go to http://www.nwfolklifefestival.org/ for added information about the festival and maps.

Anyone is welcome who plays a string instrument (violin, viola, cello or bass). Please bring a stand and arrive ahead of time.

Music is available by PDF files. For a free set of copies request these by e-mail from Judy Drake.  Even though we will have some extra parts at the event it’s best to have your own music. For more information about the event address questions to the above contact.

In case of rain.....we’re covered!  We are under a 20’ roof overhang from which tarps are also tied to enlarge the space.
